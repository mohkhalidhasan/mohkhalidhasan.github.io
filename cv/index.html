Research Areas

Wireless Security and Privacy: Physical-layer security, privacy enhancement, secure NOMA, cognitive radio, and dynamic spectrum sharing.
Intelligent Wireless Networks: UAV-assisted and RIS-enabled communications, resource allocation, spectrum sharing, and emerging 6G systems.
Trustworthy Artificial Intelligence: Federated learning, privacy-preserving learning, adversarial machine learning, and machine learning for wireless optimization.
Optical Communications: Optical camera communication, visible-light communication, hybrid optical/RF systems, and intelligent transportation applications.
